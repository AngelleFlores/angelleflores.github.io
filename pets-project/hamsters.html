Hamsters

Personality

  • Loners
  • Great at escaping
  • Like to crawl through tubes

Activities One Can Do With Hamsters

  • Watch them play with their toys
  • Handle them gently for short periods of time, since they do not typically like being held

Facts

  • Live up to 2 to 3 years
  • Nocturnal
  • Tend to bite
  • Do not like being held
  • Their teeth continually grow (need to provide hard things for them to chew on)

Diet

  • Food mix like Harry Hamster Food
  • Can have small amounts of fresh food like: carrots, cucumbers, apples with no apple seeds, and grapes
  • Treats: plain Cheerios, whole wheat toast, and sunflower seeds

Living Environment

  • Cage with solid flooring
  • Should only have one hamster per cage
  • Carefresh or Aspen bedding recommended
  • Sleeping house
  • Food bowl
  • Water bottle

Toys

  • Tubes
  • Solid exercising wheel
  • Hamster ball
  • Wooden blocks
