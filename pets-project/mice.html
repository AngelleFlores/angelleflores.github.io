Mice

Personality

  • Intelligent
  • Friendly
  • Social
  • Like to explore
  • Females get along in groups
  • Males are territorial

Activities One Can Do With Mice

  • If you have a large group of mice, they will be entertaining to watch as they eat, groom, play, and sleep together
  • Should play with them outside of their cage in a mouse-proofed area

Facts

  • Live up to 1 to 3 years
  • If handled properly, they rarely bite
  • Nocturnal

Diet

  • Lab blocks
  • Fresh food a couple of times a week like: carrots, canned tuna in spring water, cooked pasta, red grapes, and chicken
  • Treats: plain Cheerios, sunflower seeds, and millet

Living Environment

  • Mice will be happy with company, get at least two mice to live together
  • Tank/Cage
  • Carefresh bedding recommended
  • Sleeping house
  • Food bowl
  • Water bottle

Toys

  • Cardboard rolls (toilet paper rolls)
  • Solid exercising wheel
  • Rope
  • Small pet hammocks
