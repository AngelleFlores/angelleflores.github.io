About Pet Rodents

Pet rodents are interesting creatures that make wonderful pets. Most of them are social and curious, but all of them enjoy chewing on things. The teeth of rodents continually grow, so they need lots of safe chew toys to grind down their teeth. They are entertaining to watch and fun to interact with. Four types of pet rodents are gerbils, hamsters, mice, and rats.

Rats love human attention.

Hamsters like to run in a plastic ball.

Gerbils enjoy sand baths.

Mice are entertaining in large groups.

Rats can learn tricks.

Hamsters like to crawl through tubes.
